# The Conscious Contract — Should Sentient Machines Have Legal Agency?

Examines the idea of contractual AI agency: could Ava sign contracts, own assets, or be held liable? We draw parallels with corporations, minors, and animals—and ask whether law can treat an AI as both tool and actor.
